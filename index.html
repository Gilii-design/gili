Our Story

Fashion Between Classes

Us, Gildiandrick Nieuwkerk and Kyandra Berkemeijer, created this wonderful website so we can get good grades from the best teacher in the World, Mr. Losiabaar. We even named our clothing brand after him. We have 2 things in each section because we don't have any supplier's yet.

Gildiandrick - For me this was by far the best experience I ever had. Making a whole clothing website was a wonderful way to start making money, I hope atleast. I also learned how to code, so if I want to make another website in the near future, It would be alot easier.

Kyandra - I think the assignment is a good challenge that allows us to show we really learned something in our computer science class. As for the website, I think it’s nice because many people shop online nowadays, and it makes life easier for everyone.
